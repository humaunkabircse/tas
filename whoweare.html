About Us

  • TAS Group is one of the well reputed, experienced and one of the fastest growing company Bangladesh operating its business in aviation sector and achieved many milestones. We have highly experienced, educated and sincere work force which made us one of the leading organization in the aviation industry in Bangladesh.
  • Companies under TAS Aviation Group such that are currently active in operations such as Total Air Services Limited, Labbaik Travel and Tours Ltd, Total Logistics and Courier Services Ltd, Shah Travels Ltd and Sunshine Air Travel International.
  • Total Air Services Ltd., is the appointed General Sales Agent (GSA) of AirAsia Berhad for the territory in Bangladesh effective from 19th December 2014. It is also the appointed General Sales Agent (GSA) of Kuwait Always for the territory in Bangladesh effective from 1st August 2020
  • Labbaik Travel and Tours Ltd is currently representing GSA of Yemen Airways (Online & Offline) which flight is currently suspended.
  • Total Logistics and Courier Services Ltd is currently Franchisee of Smsa Express, First Flight and ZTO Express .
  • Shah Travels Ltd and Sunshine Air Travel International is currently operating in international Travel and Tourism business and involved Air Ticket selling extensively
  • The member companies of TAS group such as Accom Aviation Services Ltd., Mohammadia Aviation was previously the GSA of Oman Air, Shaheen Air (Offline), RAK Airways. Aviation Management Services (BD) Ltd. AMS, is another member company of TAS Aviation Group which introduced cargo scanning service at the International Airports in Bangladesh. It operated and managed cargo scanning services for 10 years. Eastern Logistic Ltd (ELL) is private ICD owner and operator of TAS Aviation Group, it also represented shipping agents
  • Overall, TAS Aviation Group has long experience in the Aviation industry with successful business track record and earned reputation both nationally and internationally.
  • Our Head Quarter is located in Banani, one of most prestigious area in the Capital which is next to the Diplomatic zone. We have branch office in, Gulshan 1,Dhaka, Motijheel Commercial Area, Dhaka and Branch office in Chittagong.
