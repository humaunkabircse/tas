Logistics

Total Logistics and Courier Services Ltd

Total Logistics and Courier Services Ltd (TLC), established in year the 2016, commenced its operation as the GSA of Red Cargo by Airasia. The company represented the Airline’s Cargo wing in the territory of Bangladesh from the year 2016-2018. During the time the airline uplifted approximately 3 tons of cargo per day using on 7 weekly flights using Airbus A320. Later the company diversified into express courier and freight forwarding services. Till today the company has over 50 employees across 5 offices in two major cities of Bangladesh, 5 vehicles and 4 warehouses located near the major airport and seaport of the country.

Estern Logistics Ltd.

Eastern Logistics Limited a private inland container deport situated on a 16 Acre land at Katghar, North Patenga, Chittagong adjacent to Chittagong port the biggest sea port in Bangladesh. The company has the capability and capacity to store and handle roughly 15,000-20,000 TEUs per day. The firm is equipped will all necessary equipments needed to efficiently and effectively maintain its operation. The company have weight scales, 20 prime mover, 4 reach stackers, 3 side stackers and etc.

Managment Services

Aviation Managment Services Ltd

Aviation Management Services (BD) Ltd. (AMS) of TAS Aviation Group is the pioneer company in introducing cargo scanning facility at the international airports in Bangladesh. In response to the Gov’t decision in 2002 to make it mandatory for all exportable cargo to be scanned before departure. AMS offered the most efficient and cost effective sate of the art equipment and systems to be installed and operated in the country’s international airports. The service level of AMS has since then been widely acknowledged to be of international quality and standard by the leading airlines using these airports including Saudia, Emirates, Gulf Air, Yemen Airways, Singapore Airlines, Malaysian Airlines, Thai Airways, Indian Airlines, Qata Airways, Kuwait Air etc.
